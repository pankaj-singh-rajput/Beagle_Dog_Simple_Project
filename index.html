The Beagle Blog

The Beagle is a breed of small scent hound, similar in appearance to the much
larger foxhound.The beagle was developed primarily for hunting hare, know as
beagling. Possessing a great sense of smell and superior tracking instincts, the
beagle is the primary breed used a detection dog for prohibited agricultural
imports and foodstuffs in quarantine around the world. The beagle is intelligent
and is a popular pet due to its size, good temper, and a lack of inherted health
problems.

Trivia

  1. Height - 13-16 in (33-41cm)
  2. Weight - 22-25 ib (10.0-11.3)
  3. Coat -Short haired, hard coat of medium length
  4. Colour -Tricolor or white in combination with black & tan/brown or brown/tan
  5. Life span 12-15 years
